Mike G's Favorite Films of All Time


Captain America: The Winter Solider

Top 5 Marvel Film of all time. Has great action, suspense, acting, all the main ingredentiets of a superhero film


Avengers: Endgame

Greatest Ending of a Movie Saga. Has amazing callbacks, great dialogue, and memorable moments. The MCU could have ended there imo


Mulan

My favorite Disney movie of all time. Great coming of age story, strong female lead w/o being blantant about her gender and amazing music. Easy rewatch anytime.


Top Gun: Maverick

Same for Endgame in a way. Has great callback to the original but amazing stunt work that is unparralled


Gone Girl

Thriller that shocked me
